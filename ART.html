Cosmere Fanarts

Life before death, strength before weakness, journey before destination

I recently became interested in a book series called The Cosmere by Brandon Sanderson, and I am currently working on a series of illustrations depicting the most pivotal story points of its three main characters.

Every illustration in this series follows a more intricate and detailed version of my usual artistic process. I begin with a rough sketch on paper to capture the core composition and idea. Next, I create a more refined version, adding precision to the details before scanning it into digital format. Once scanned, I use Clip Studio Paint to ink the drawing, ensuring clean and dynamic linework. From there, I apply flat colors to establish the base palette, followed by adding shadows, lighting, and textures to bring depth, atmosphere, and realism to the final piece. This method allows me to carefully craft each illustration while exploring new levels of complexity in my art.

Anime Fanarts

Every Life Tells a Unique Story

Anime captivates through its ability to craft unique narratives, turning seemingly ordinary moments—such as a high school volleyball match—into events of profound significance and emotion. Simultaneously, it breathes life into fantastical worlds, making them feel both immersive and familiar. Each story and character unfolds with purpose, offering lessons and perspectives that resonate deeply and linger long after the journey ends.

One of the anime/manga that has influenced me the most recently is Blue Lock. Beyond its compelling story, which captures the thrill of seizing opportunities in the moment and the importance of striving for one’s goals, it stands out for Yusuke Nomura’s masterful artistry. His work perfectly conveys the raw emotions of the characters—the euphoria, the tension, and the desperation—bringing every moment to life with unparalleled intensity.
